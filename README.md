# New Feature

I added progress bars into my website to showcase my various completion status for the games I play.

- Wizard101 is based on `Completed Worlds`
- Warframe is based on the highest mastery tier available. As of writing, it is currently Legendary 6 (MR 36)
- Dungeon Defenders 2 is based on the amount of times I have ascended out of 50
- Terraria is based on achievements on the platform Steam
- Stardew Valley is based on achievements on the platform Steam
